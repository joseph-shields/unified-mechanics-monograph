# Freeze Manifest
### Unified Mechanics · The Corpus · 2026

This edition is a rework for reading: one monograph and one companion standard. The mathematics is unchanged from the earlier three-monograph freeze; the telling was rewritten in a plain, unfolding voice, and the standalone application tests were folded into Universopedia as an honestly flagged exploratory record. The superseded editions are preserved byte-exact in `Archive/`.

## What moved, and where

- The three-monograph editions (A World of Distinctions Main and Compact, The Closure, the Authority Note) are in `Archive/Superseded editions/`, unchanged.
- The standalone application tests (01 through 04) are in `Research Archive/Systems of Interpretation — detailed workings/`. Their honest summary is the Systems of Interpretation part of Universopedia.
- The earlier one-page results ledger and the earlier navigation guides are in `Archive/Superseded editions/`, superseded by this READ ME and by Systems of Interpretation.
- `Papers as Issued/` is unchanged: the full technical derivations behind the Monograph's later movements live there in complete rigour.

## Discipline

Corrections and continuations belong in new papers, not in silent edits to a frozen file. Nothing in `Archive/` governs the current corpus; it is kept for provenance only.
